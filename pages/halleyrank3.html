3. Butterfly Day by Day

Alternate Name(s): Hibi Chouchou
Shibazeki Suiren is a stunningly beautiful girl. Her classmates are astonished by her beauty, but after noticing her quiet manners and lack of contact except with her friends, she receives the nickname "Takane no Hana", a beautiful flower but impossible to reach. However, after a fellow freshman called Kawasumi Kouha saves her from a persistent suitor, she finds herself mesmerized by him… as he's the only boy who won't look at her no matter what.

This series is really sweet and cute. Both Suiren and Kouha are pretty shy, though Suiren more than him, and I love reading their interactions. They have a very wholesome and innocent love. It's very heart warming to see them develop and take the courage to communicate their feelings. A CLASSIC.
