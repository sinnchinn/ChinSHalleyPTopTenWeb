#10: AO NO FLAG

AUTHOR: KAITO

Taichi Ichinose used to be friends with Toma Mita in elementary school. As Toma's popularity grew, their friendship shrank. This distance between them continued until the beginning of their third year of high school when the perpetually shy and timid Futaba Kuze approaches Taichi.

This manga was a lot different than other romance mangas I've read. The plot has a more slice of life feeling to it but it was a really good read. The ending completely shocked me like it shocked most of it's viewers. It's a really wholesome manga that I would recommend anyone to read!

Where to Read: Mangadex

Where to Watch: No anime yet.
