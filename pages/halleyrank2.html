2. Loving Yamada-kun at Lv999

Alternative Name(s): Yamada-kun to Lv999 no Koi wo Suru
Synopsis: Recently dumped, Akane is just about to quit the game she used to play with her boyfriend when she meets Yamada in the same RPG. Yamada in real life turns out to be somewhat of a legend. The only problem is - he is ONLY interested in the game. As she encounters him more and more, Akane's feelings start to grow.

ANOTHER WHOLESOME ONE, WOW. This one isn't as heart-wrenching and emotionally hurting me as the others. I find it very entertaining when they bring gaming terminology. It's also funny when Yamada has to teach Akane how to play a game. Overall very cute, and I especially LOVE the art style.
