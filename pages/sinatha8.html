#8: STROBE EDGE

AUTHOR: SAKISAKA IO

15-year-old Ninako Kinoshita has never been in love until an encounter on the train with her school's most popular boy, the quiet Ren Ichinose. As she grows closer to Ren, she falls in love with him, but must learn to cope with the unfortunate fact that Ren already has a longtime girlfriend.

This was one of the first mangas I read growing up and it really holds a special place in my heart. What caught my attention the most was the art style which matters to me a lot when choosing a manga to read. The heroine is new to love and it's fun watching her find her first ever love and tries to navigate through her feelings.

Where to Read: Mangadex
