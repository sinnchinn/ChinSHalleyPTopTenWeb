#7: AO HARU RIDE

AUTHOR: SAKISAKA IO

Futaba Yoshioka is a 16-year-old high school student who attempts to fit in with her female friends by acting "unfeminine", due to a history her female classmates ostracizing her out of jealousy. As a result, she feels her life is uninteresting.

This manga has the same author as the previous manga, I really liked their past work. This is another manga where it follows the heroine's first love but with a lot of turbulations. I think I've read this manga about 10 times. At the time it came out, it was a really fresh and new story with a strong heroine.

Where to Read: Mangadex

Where to Watch: Crunchyroll
