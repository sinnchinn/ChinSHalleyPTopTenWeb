7. Fruits Basket

Synopsis: After Tohru is taken in by the Soma family, she learns that twelve family members transform involuntarily into animals of the Chinese zodiac and helps them deal with the emotional pain caused by the transformations.

The plot is a bit different compared to my normal slice of life, shoujo Anime/Mangas I'm used to, but it's well developed and very intriguing. The anime's art style is so beautiful matched with the story line tugging at MY heartstring! The reason why it's kind of lower in ranks is because it was a bit slow Season 1 and it took me awhile to get into.
