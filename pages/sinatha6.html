#6: THE MOON THAT RISES IN THE DAY

AUTHOR: HEYUM

The series tells the story of a man who is stuck in time after being killed by his lover, and a woman who has no memories of her past life and experiences time continuously

This manga absolutely destroyed my heart. It's such a sad story and really showcases a right person wrong time love trope. It has a bit of horror aspects also, which is a genre I don't really gravitate towards. Nonetheless, this manga is extremely heart breaking and I couldn't even finish the last 3 chapters because I knew where it was going and it broke my heart that much.

Where to Read: Mangadex
