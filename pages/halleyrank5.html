5. My Happy Marriage

Alternate Name(s): Watashi no Shiawase na Kekkon
Synopsis: Despite being born into a family with unusual powers in its bloodline, Saimori Miyo had not inherited any special abilities. Moreover, she was treated like a lowly servant by her half-sister and even unloved and shunned by her parents. Miyo was a forgotten daughter. Seen as nothing more than a hindrance, Miyo is married off to the Kudou family. To a man rumored to be cold and ruthlessâ€¦

Saimori Miyo's family is SO irritating. Because of all the abuse she went through, it makes it better and all the more when her arranged marriage partner, Kudou Kiyoka slowly falls in love. I love that Kudou stands up for Saimori and treats her like she deserves!
