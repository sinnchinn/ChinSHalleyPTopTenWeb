#5: BOKURA GA ITA

AUTHOR: AI YAZAWA

Nanami Takahashi is a high school student entering her first year. Immediately on her first day however, she becomes the victim of a practical joke by the school's most popular guy, Motoharu Yano, who coincidentally also ends up in her class.

I watched the anime to this manga first but fell in love with it and decided to read the manga. This is a manga that I really hold dear to my heart because I love the characters so much. This follows the heroine and her first love from high school into their adult lives. It broke my heart in a lot of the story telling but I think it really showcased first loves really well.

Where to Read: Mangadex

Where to Watch: Amazon
